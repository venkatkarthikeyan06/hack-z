About the Platform

The proposed solution is A personalized adaptive mastering platform designed to address the diverse instructional needs of neurodiverse and special-needs students, including individuals with ADHD, autism, and dyslexia.

This platform leverages to create personalized learning profiles for each student by capturing data on their cognitive, sensory, and emotional responses.

Features

Multi-modal Content

Videos, interactive simulations, gamified tasks, and audio-based training.

Customizable Interface

Interface to match sensory preferences.

Supportive Features

Text-to-speech, speech-to-text, and dyslexia-friendly fonts.

Benefits

By tailoring the learning experience to individual needs, the platform fosters a supportive atmosphere where students can thrive academically and personally.
